Ever visited an ugly, hard-to-look-at site? Re-SS parses all the visible characters in a site, animates them, and rearranges them into a complex art form! Draw your own picture beforehand or choose from our gallery and let Re-SS give that ugly site a makeover! Available as a Chrome and Firefox-supported bookmarklet.

Re-SS was our entry for the Fall 2013 Y-Hack at Yale University. Authored by @alec-heif, @paerhati, and William Wong.
